This page presents a digitized object from the HathiTrust Digital Library. It focuses on The Crisis magazine and the poem “Retribution” by Ada B. Luckie.

Cover of The Crisis

Poem: “Retribution” by Ada B. Luckie

Description

The Crisis was an African American magazine that documented social, political, and cultural issues in the early twentieth century along with other news and works from African Americans. The poem “Retribution” has themes of injustice and resistance during this period.
